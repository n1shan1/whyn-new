05 — Voice & tone
whyn sounds like a senior developer who's seen too many broken funnels. Direct. Specific. No enthusiasm-speak. Every line earns its place.
Direct
Answer first, context second. No preamble.
Specific
Real numbers, real timelines, real scope.
Honest
Says what's out of scope. No oversell.
Write like this

"Growth tier starts at ₹25K. That's a full multi-page site, SEO setup, lead capture, and Cal.com booking. Live in 4–5 weeks."

Pricing question — direct, specific, no fluff

"Your site probably isn't the problem. The problem is what happens after someone lands on it. No capture. No follow-up. Cold lead."

Problem framing — names the real issue, creates urgency

"We don't build apps or e-commerce. We build lead systems. If you need a Shopify store, we'll point you to someone good."

Out of scope — honest, no apology, redirects cleanly
Never write this
"We craft bespoke digital experiences that elevate your brand." ✗
"Our passionate team leverages cutting-edge technology to unlock your potential." ✗
"We're a full-service digital agency for forward-thinking businesses." ✗
"Great question! I'd be happy to help you with that today." ✗
"Results may vary based on your individual business circumstances." ✗
